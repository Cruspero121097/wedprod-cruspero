Welcome to Bohol!

Thank you for choosing Bohol as your vacation destination!

Bohol is an island province located in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines. It is famous for its stunning natural attractions, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality.

Key Features:

  • Chocolate Hills: One of the most iconic landmarks of Bohol, the Chocolate Hills are a geological formation consisting of around 1,260 hills spread over an area of more than 50 square kilometers.
  • Tarsier Sanctuary: Bohol is home to the world's smallest primates, the Philippine tarsiers. Visitors can observe these fascinating creatures in their natural habitat at various sanctuaries across the island.
  • White Sand Beaches: Bohol boasts pristine white sand beaches, including Alona Beach and Panglao Island, where visitors can relax, swim, and enjoy water sports such as snorkeling and diving.
  • Historical Sites: Explore Bohol's rich history by visiting historical sites such as the Blood Compact Shrine, Baclayon Church, and the centuries-old Spanish watchtowers.
  • Local Cuisine: Indulge in Bohol's delicious local cuisine, which includes fresh seafood, traditional Filipino dishes, and exotic delicacies like balut (duck embryo).

Plan Your Visit:

Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, Bohol offers something for everyone. Plan your visit today and experience the beauty and charm of this enchanting island paradise!
